Name: CustomApp_Swing_JavaFX_STK_X_Map_View
Vendor: Analytical Graphics, Inc.
Description: This is an example of a STK Engine Custom Application using Sun Microsystems's Java AWT/Swing/JavaFX framework. It demonstrates the code needed to change the view of the STKX Map control.
Directions: 1. Press the Scenario -> New Scenario menu item.
2. Did you notice the JavaFX View Toolbar fades in and translates from the top of the UI.
3. Click the Zoom In button with image of a magnifying glass and plus sign.
4. Did you notice the custom animation of the Zoom In button?
5. Left mouse click and hold the map and drag down and right to create a "zoom box". Release the left mouse button.
6. Click the Pan button with image of a human hand.
7. Did you notice the custom animation of the Pan button?
8. Left click and hold the map while pulling the mouse left and right.
9. Click the Zoom Out button with image of a magnifying glass and minus sign.
10. Did you notice the custom animation of the Zoom Out button?
11. Press the Scenario -> Close Scenario menu item.
12. Did you notice the JavaFX View Toolbar fades out and translates to the top of the UI.
